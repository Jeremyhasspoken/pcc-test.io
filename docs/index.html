COMING FROM WAIKIKI? LET US DO THE DRIVING

We’ll take you on a tour of Oahu’s most spectacular beachside highway. Transportation packages include a round trip to the Center in an air-conditioned coach, with your journey narrated by a local guide.

MOTOR COACH - $27.00

(Standard Transportation)

  • Pick up at the designated location near your Waikiki hotel

  • Ride in a luxurious top-of-the-line motor coach

  • Trip narrated by friendly, local guide

  • Two pickup times: 10 a.m. and noon.

  • Return to Waikiki approximately 10:30 p.m.

MINI COACH - $37.00

(Curbside Service)

  • Pickup right from the lobby of your Waikiki hotel

  • More personal experience aboard our comfortable mini-coach

  • Trip narrated by a friendly local guide

  • Pickup time: 10 a.m.

  • Return to Waikiki approximately 10:30 p.m.

Transportation is included with Circle Island Packages, or can be purchased separately. When booking online, you can add your transportation option during check-out.
